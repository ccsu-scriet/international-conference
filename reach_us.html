How to reach Meerut

Meerut City: Connectivity

Meerut, a major city in Uttar Pradesh, India, is well-connected by air, rail, and road.

By Air

Meerut does not have a commercial airport. The nearest airport is the Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi.

By Train

Meerut Junction is a major railway station with trains connecting to cities across India.

By Road

The city is accessible via national highways and is well-served by state and private buses. It's also convenient to reach by car from nearby cities. Local transport options include auto-rickshaws, taxis, and buses.
